Pixelmon Generations IV Calculator

If Pixelmon Extras ever becomes unreliable, this calculator will accurately estimate the individual values of your pokemon. Due to floating-point rounding, only a range [min/max] of values can be produced. In general, the higher your pokemon's level, the more precise the results.

If you need to use this calculator, that probably means that you do not have access to the /evs command. Make sure you keep track of the effort values of your pokemon! These values are necessary for the calculations.

To use this calculator, fill out the fields with the correct information from your pokemon's summary in-game. Then click "Calculate IVs", and the results will render at the bottom of the page.

I made this tool in a few hours and it is not well tested. If you find a bug or other issue, or have feedback, let me know @ DThaiPome#0789!


Pokemon Species

Note: For pokemon with alternate forms that affect their base stats, the species should be entered as [pokemon]-[form].

Examples: "deoxys-normal", "giratina-origin", "rotom-wash"

For more information, check out PokeAPI, the service used to get data on pokemon base stats, or contact me and I can help :)
